How Much Does It Cost to Repipe a House in 2026?

Published on July 2026 • 5 min read

If your home was built before 1995, you may be dealing with galvanized steel or polybutylene pipes that are rusting from the inside out. Re-piping your entire house is a major home improvement project, but understanding costs beforehand prevents budget surprises.

Average Cost Breakdown by Home Size

In 2026, the average national cost to repipe a single-family house ranges from $4,500 to $12,500. Here is how square footage impacts the final quote:

  • 1,000 – 1,500 sq ft (1-2 Baths): $3,800 – $6,200
  • 1,500 – 2,500 sq ft (2-3 Baths): $5,500 – $9,200
  • 2,500+ sq ft (3+ Baths, Multiple Stories): $8,500 – $14,000+
