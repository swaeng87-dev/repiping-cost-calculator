Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to House Repiping Cost Calculator ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our website and interactive cost calculator.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our cost calculator or contact forms, we may collect:

  • Property Details: ZIP code, home square footage, number of stories, foundation type, and fixture counts entered into the calculator.
  • Contact Information: Full name, email address, phone number, and street address when you request a detailed quote.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address, device information, and site navigation patterns via cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected to:

  • Generate accurate, real-time home repiping estimates based on regional data.
  • Forward your service requests and contact details (via swarupm@zohomail.com and partner channels) to licensed local plumbing professionals who can provide your official quote.
  • Communicate with you regarding your inquiry, customer support, or updates.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at swarupm@zohomail.com.
